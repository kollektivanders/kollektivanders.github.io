About us

We value the thirst for knowledge, creation
& communication. We attach great
importance to peaceful interaction within
our team and with our clients.

KollektivAnders is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Berlin. Through the union of 2 multidisciplinary designers, the range of projects and specializations is large.

The design studio was founded by us Alexander Kunau. We both graduated in media informatics and were able to explore and acquire through the combination of both core areas. Our specializations run on graphic design, motion design, 3D visualizations and frontend programming.
