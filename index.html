About Us

We are a multidisciplinary digital design studio reimagining
how people connect with brands, design and technology.

KollektivAnders is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Berlin. Through the union of 2 multidisciplinary designers, the range o f projects and specializations is large.

The design studio was founded by us Alexander Kunau and Erik Gottschall. We both graduated in media informatics and were able to explore and acquire through the combination of both core areas. Our specializations run on corporate design, graphic design, motion design, 3D visualizations and frontend programming.
